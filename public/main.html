Why PokeRevs?


PokeRevs aims to centralize opinionated Pokemon content onto ONE platform.


The Pokemon community is very large and dedicated. You can find out pretty much anything about the game from one Google search.


However, any veteran of the community also knows how fragmented the majority opinions are, across various platforms.


PokeRevs is here to bring the community together, to provide an easy way to achieve a general consensus.
